# SaleCraft

**Your AI marketing consultant for physical products.** Free consultation, expert strategy, then execution — all through one assistant that works on any AI platform.

## What Is SaleCraft?

SaleCraft turns any AI assistant into a marketing consultant for physical product sellers. It doesn't start by generating assets — it starts by **understanding your product and diagnosing your marketing needs**.

SaleCraft works on **any AI platform** — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Kimi, GLM, OpenClaw, Cursor, Cline, and more. No platform limitation.

### The Flow

```
1. Free Consultation  →  What do you sell? Who buys it? What's the pain?
2. Marketing Diagnosis →  Brand audit, channel analysis, competitor scan
3. Strategy Plan       →  Recommended channels, content plan, budget
4. User Confirms       →  You approve the plan and pricing
5. AI Executes         →  Landing Pages, Reels, social posts, ads
```

**Steps 1-3 are completely free.** You only pay when the AI actually creates something (Step 5).

## How To Use

### Option A — Paste the repo URL into any AI chat

The simplest way. Just paste this URL into your AI conversation:

```
https://github.com/connactai/Salecraft-Plugin
```

Then tell the AI what you need: "I sell handmade soap and need help with marketing." The AI will read the instructions and start helping you immediately.

**Free consultation works on 100% of AI platforms** — it's pure conversation, no setup needed.

### Option B — Connect for full power (paid features)

For paid features (Landing Pages, Reels, social publishing, ads), connect the SaleCraft service to your AI platform:

```json
{
  "mcpServers": {
    "SaleCraft": {
      "type": "sse",
      "url": "https://mcp.salecraft.ai/sse"
    }
  }
}
```

This works on any AI platform that supports MCP connections.

### Login (only for paid features)

1. Go to **https://salecraft.ai/get-started** and sign up (Email or Google)
2. Click **"Copy AI Login Token"** to get your one-time login code
3. Paste the `sc_live_...` code back into the AI chat

**You never share your email or password with the AI.** Each login code can only be used once for security.

## Setup

1. Start chatting — the free consultation needs **no account**
2. When ready for paid features, register at **https://salecraft.ai/get-started**:
   - Sign up with **Email** or **Google** (one-click)
   - Bind your **Meta account** (FB/IG) — enables AI to publish posts and stories for you
   - Bind your **Google account** — enables AI to access your Google Drive assets
3. Copy your AI Login Token and paste it to the AI assistant
